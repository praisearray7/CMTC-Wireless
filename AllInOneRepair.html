Common Issues & Solutions

Extremely Slow / Freezing

Most AIOs use slow HDDs. Upgrading to an SSD makes them 10x faster.

Loud Fan Noise

Dust accumulates easily in AIOs. We open them up and clean the cooling system.

Cracked Screen Glass

We can replace just the glass on some models, or the full display assembly.

Won't Turn On

Could be the external power brick or internal power supply. We test both.

Touchscreen Not Working

Driver corruption or digitizer failure. We diagnose and repair the touch layer.

Boot Loops / Blue Screen

Operating system corruption is common. We can repair Windows or macOS.
